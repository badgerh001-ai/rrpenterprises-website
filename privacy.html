Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At RRP Enterprises, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and logistics data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide efficient logistics services, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, Company Name, Email Address, Phone Number, and GSTIN.
  • Logistics Data: Pickup/Delivery addresses, Cargo details (type, weight, volume), and Consignee details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information when you access our tracking system.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for business purposes:

  • Processing FTL and Bulk Cargo bookings.
  • Providing real-time shipment tracking updates.
  • Generating invoices and E-Way bills as per GST regulations.
  • Communicating operational updates or delays.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, secure servers) to protect your data. Access to logistics data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Trusted fleet partners (drivers/transporters) solely for fulfillment.
  • Government authorities (e.g., GST officers, Checkposts) if legally required for transit compliance.
